Common Football Injuries

  • Sprains and strains
  • Knee injuries (ACL, MCL tears)
  • Concussions
  • Shoulder injuries
  • Ankle injuries

Injury Prevention Tips

  1. Warm up properly before practice and games
  2. Stay hydrated
  3. Use proper tackling techniques
  4. Strengthen muscles through conditioning
  5. Improve flexibility with regular stretching
  6. Follow the rules of the game
  7. Don't play through pain - seek medical attention

Proper Equipment

  • Well-fitting helmet with face mask
  • Mouth guard
  • Shoulder pads
  • Hip pads and tailbone protector
  • Knee pads
  • Thigh guards
  • Cleats appropriate for the playing surface

Remember: Safety first! Always consult with coaches and medical professionals for personalized advice.
